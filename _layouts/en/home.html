A State Startup is not just an oxymoron, but a match between a team and a mission. There is no capital investment, no separate juridical entity, only the usage of the tools and practices of the digital startups to solve a friction in an interaction an administration has with citizens.

Find out more!

How State Startups work

  1. A civil servant investigates a pain point affecting citizens dealing with government.
  2. A team of two to four builds a product in under six months using modern technologies.
  3. The team scales the service out by meeting the needs of a broader user population.
  4. A public sector body assumes ownership of the product and continuously improves it.
